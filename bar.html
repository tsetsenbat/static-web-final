Nights in Budapest

25 April 2022 3 Min read

Budapest's raving nightlife

When someone comes to Budapest, they often might picture the mesmerising architecture or the city's significance as a historical landmark. But Budapest is not limited to these, it is a city of culture, people, and most importantly, fun. The city is a place where people can enjoy themselves and interact with each other.

The party district is largely between the Deák Ferenc tér-Oktogon-Blaha Lujza tér triangle where the number of clubs, bars and restaurants exceed 800. Király is the main strip, but every single street is home to dozens of bars.

Szimpla

Huge pub with old mismatched items & a disused Trabant car, with music, food, market & a garden.

Instant

Large entertainment venue with theatrical decor, plus 18 bars, 6 dance floors & 2 gardens.

Morrison's 2

Cellar nightspot with bars, 4 dance floors with techno & disco, karaoke & all-year heated garden.

Mazel Tov

Light, airy cafe & cultural space with garden, serving bar snacks, sandwiches, beers & hot drinks.

Ötkert

Busy bar serving food plus big, informal club with DJs, live bands & open-air dance floor & terrace.

Arzenál

Night club in Budapest, playing mostly techno & house music. Good spot for raves.

Enjoy the nightlife in Budapest, but remember to respect the locals and party responsibly.
