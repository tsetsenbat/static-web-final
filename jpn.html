Taste of Japan in Hungary

25 April 2022 3 Min read

Japanese cuisine in Budapest

Japanese cuisine is undoubtedly one of the greatest culinary traditions in the world. It is a fusion of the traditional elements of the Japanese cuisine with the modern elements of the modern world. Not only do Japanese people love it, the food is also very popular in the world. Here in Budapest, Hungary, we look at the restaurants that give us the best experience of eating Japanese food.

Komachi Bistro

Komachi Bistro is a Japanese restaurant located in the center of Budapest. It is a popular place for Japanese food. Their ramen and curry sets are quite famous among its visitors, not to mention the quality of the food.

WAFU

WAFU is located in the center of Budapest, next to the popular Szimpla Kert. Their ramen is not as great as Komachi's, but it is still quite good. The food is very tasty and the prices are quite reasonable. Definitely try the karaage!

DON DOKO DON

Famous for its simple yet filling dishes, DON DOKO DON is a cozy bistro located next to Kalvin ter. Their curry don is a big favorite among their customers, and the service is always friendly. The prices are quite reasonable, with a 10% discount for students! If you need a quick and rejuvenating lunch, try DON DOKO DON!

Japan Okonomiyaki Kincsan

A small restaurant which specializes in Okonomiyaki, Japan Okonomiyaki Kincsan is located on Raday street. It is a popular place for okonomiyaki, and the prices are great. The cooks prepare the dishes in front of the customers, and they are always friendly. If you are looking for an authentic Japanese spot to eat some okonomiyaki, try Japan Okonomiyaki Kincsan!

Here's our ranking below!
Ranking Restaurant
1 Komachi Bistro
2 DON DOKO DON
3 WAFU
4 Japan Okonomiyaki Kincsan

We hope you were able to find a place you'd like to eat in someday. We are always happy to spread the love of Japanese cuisine!
